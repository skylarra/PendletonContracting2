About Us

Pendleton Contracting was founded by Austin Chapman, a passionate and skilled contractor with a vision to provide exceptional service and quality craftsmanship to the upstate South Carolina area. Inspired by a rich history of family trade and a deep love for the community, Austin saw an opportunity to create a contracting business that prioritizes the unique needs and desires of each customer. With over 8 years of experience in the industry, Austin knew that Pendleton Contracting could deliver unparalleled service in lawn care, landscaping, plumbing, concrete, and tree services.
